5 fun cat facts

  • Whisker superpowers – A cat's whiskers aren't just for looks! They help cats measure spaces and detect vibrations in the air.
  • Purring mystery – Cats purr not only when they're happy, but also when they're stressed or healing. The frequency of their purrs may even promote bone healing!
  • Cat naps add up – Cats sleep around 12–16 hours a day, meaning by age 9, a cat has already spent about 6 years sleeping.
  • Meow just for humans – Adult cats typically only meow to communicate with humans, not other cats.
  • Super jumpers – Cats can jump up to six times their body length in one leap, thanks to their powerful hind legs.
